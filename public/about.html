About Us

The Official Student Publication of the University of Mindanao Ilang High School

Welcome to The Maroon Ink, the official student-led news organization of the University of Mindanao (UM) Ilang High School. As the primary chronicler of our campus history, we are dedicated to delivering relevant news, insightful features, and a platform for creative student expression.

1. Our Identity

The name The Maroon Ink represents our deep-rooted connection to the University of Mindanao and our commitment to the power of the written word. We serve as a vital link between the school administration, the faculty, and the student body, ensuring that the voice of the UM Ilang youth is heard and respected.

2. Mission and Vision

Our publication is guided by the following core principles:

  • Mission: To provide accurate, fair, and timely information while fostering journalistic excellence and critical thinking among our students.
  • Vision: To become a premier campus publication that empowers student leaders and serves as a catalyst for positive change within the UM community.

3. Editorial Scope

Our coverage spans across various facets of school life, including but not limited to:

  • Campus News: Reporting on school policies, local events, and student government activities.
  • Feature Stories: Highlighting the unique talents, achievements, and narratives of our students and faculty.
  • Opinion and Editorial: Offering a space for balanced discourse on issues that affect the youth and the community.
  • Sports News Documenting the victories of our athletes and our high-achievers.

4. Journalistic Integrity

The Maroon Ink operates under the guidance of our Faculty Advisers and the school administration. We adhere to the highest standards of journalism, prioritizing truth, accuracy, and fairness in every article, photograph, and illustration we publish.
