MASKS AND MEMORIES | Junior and Senior Promenade 2026 Shines at Apo View...

By: Khezhea Gumubao & John Stephen Gabisan
·
Tue Mar 03 2026 11:41:14 GMT+0800 (Philippine Standard Time)

𝗠𝗔𝗦𝗞𝗦 𝗔𝗡𝗗 𝗠𝗘𝗠𝗢𝗥𝗜𝗘𝗦 | Junior and Senior Promenade 2026 Shines at Apo View Hotel

The much-anticipated Junior and Senior Promenade was held on March 2, 2026, at the historic Apo View Hotel in Davao City. With the theme “Masquerade,” the event gathered Grade 10 and Grade 12 students for an evening of mystery, elegance, and celebration.

The program featured a red carpet parade, the symbolic turnover of the Key of Responsibility, lighting of candles, a graceful cotillion, and a dinner. It continued with the reading of the class prophecy, token giving, a modern dance, and an awarding ceremony—highlighting both tradition and creativity in one unforgettable night.

The celebration ended on a high note as everyone sang “Don’t Stop Believin’” by Journey, bringing the magical evening to a heartfelt and memorable close.
