Selected participants of the 2024 Inter-Branch Expression Skills Competition of UM Ilang High School to be held at UM Panabo College, upcoming April 30 presents their respective events, Asian Parliamentary Debate, Vocal Solo, Vocal Duet and Hip-Hop Dance on April 18, 2024.

Aside from the aforementioned onstage competitions, UMIHS's participants for offstage competitions including the Archimedes Math Quiz Bee, Mobile Journalism, Collaborative Writing, and Poster Making Contest are also preparing for their quest towards excellence and victory in the upcoming Inter-Branch Expression Skills Competition 2024.
