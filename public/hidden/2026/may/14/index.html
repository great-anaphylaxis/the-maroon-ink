Sa susunod na habang buhay

So daghan man ug broken diri, someone might need this. Sakit jud, diba? We all long that pure love and affection from a special someone. Yung someone na pipiliin ka araw araw, yung someone na by your side when your at your lowest, yung someone na hindi ka iiwan in every hour of your life. Yung akala niyo na mag wo-work out kayo, yung this is someone that is perfect for me talaga. But not every sweet relationship will end happily ever after. In fact, mostly jud kay naga end with the two parties being hurt.

Sakit jud siya, and the more you love someone, the more it hurts in your heart na mawala siya. But, not all partners are meant to be. That person might be a lesson for you, a reminder to align yourself with what matters the most. That person might be a sign to first prepare yourself before entering a relationship, or yung uunahin mo muna sarili mo before mo ibibigay lahat sa taong hindi naman para kayo sa isa't isa. That breakup might me a head's up for you to be more careful in loving someone again.

Sure, painful pa siya karun, but pain deserves to be felt. It's temporary, but it's valuable. For now, if you feel pain, focus on yourself sa jud. Fix yourself, rebuild slowly. If you have made that decision, try to move on jud. Commit to it. Dili kaayo siya lisud if naa siyay sakit nga gibuhat or something, but lisud kaayo mag move on if you really love that person, and that is how our heart works. Please, don't shower yourself in pain. You are a better person than this. Your world doesn't depend on him/her.

If gusto paka mubalik, but you're feeling in pain right now, this is maybe the sign to let that person go. Dili paka ready. Dili pamo duha committed. True love doesn't give you butterflies, it gives you peace. Love motivated by that "spark" doesn't last long, sa sugod ra jud na siya taman. Fast and easy love is shallow. Love is much sweeter when it is built through things beyond that "spark" or butterfly feeling.

And this is very important. If pwede, ayaw sa jud mo ug sulod ug isa ka relationship na dili mu ready duha. Most importantly, do not enter if dili mo committed duha. Sure, masakitan jud mo ana. It's better to stay friends (or close friends) nalang jud, without the feelings and romanticism.

Don't worry guys, this is part of life. Masakitan ug masakitan jud ta, no matter how much we love that person, if dili siya para sa imoha, then dili jud. The important thing is to improve kung asa ka nagkulang, and apply it so that next time, if makita na nimo ang right person for you, you can love that person all you want and be confident na hindi ka niya talaga iiwan. I hope na makita jud ninyo nang someone na pipiliin ka sa habang buhay.
